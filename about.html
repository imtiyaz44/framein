ABOUT US

The inception of Frame In dates back to 2016, envisioned by the architects Samantha Hossain Sheetal and Umayed Samee. The ambition was to redefine the essence of living spaces through integrated, tailored and personalized interior design solutions.

While staying attuned to design trends, we also honor classical and traditional aspects, ensuring that our spaces evolve while remaining grounded in enduring beauty. We specialize in weaving together contemporary aesthetics with timeless design principles, creating interior spaces that resonate with both modernism and tradition. Our celebration of modernist sensibilities blended with heritage results in captivating interiors that tell stories.

We curated a sense of historical continuity by incorporating bricks that have weathered two centuries, sourced from deconstructed buildings. These bricks were strategically placed to serve as a narrative of the home's connection to the past.

Our commitment to serve better quality to our clients is the one of the core motto of our organization.
The journey at Frame In is a collaborative effort, shared with our dedicated team, valued clients, and partners. Together, we strive to elevate lifestyles, ensuring that each step we take is in harmony with the well-being and aspirations of those we serve. Discover the FRAME IN difference where innovation meets tradition, and lifestyle finds its expression within four walls.
